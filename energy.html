Green Technology

Green technology, also known as environmental technology or clean technology, aims to mitigate or reverse the impact of human activity on the environment.

Key aspects of green technology include:

  • Renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydroelectric power.
  • Energy-efficient technologies for buildings and transportation.
  • Waste reduction and recycling innovations.
  • Water purification and conservation solutions.

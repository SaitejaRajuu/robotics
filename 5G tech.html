5G Technology

5G technology represents the fifth generation of mobile networks, offering significant advancements over its predecessors.

Key features of 5G include:

  • Ultra-fast speeds up to 10 Gbps.
  • Low latency, enabling real-time applications.
  • Massive connectivity for IoT and smart devices.
  • Enhanced reliability and network slicing capabilities.

For robotics, 5G opens up opportunities for enhanced real-time control, improved coordination in swarm robotics, and expanded capabilities in autonomous vehicles.
